About Me

I like to think of myself as an energetic, creative, empathetic, and intellectually curious teacher and writer. I have a PhD in English, and for the last thirty years, I have taught various English, humanities, developmental writing, reading, integrated reading and writing, critical thinking, and college success courses. I've been involved in online teaching since 1999, and have designed and taught courses online every year since then. I've authored several textbooks published by McGraw-Hill Higher Education and have spoken nationally on topics related to teaching in higher education, focusing on creating engagement and deep learning in online courses.
